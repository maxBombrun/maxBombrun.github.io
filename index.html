Employment

  • January 2018 - Present

    Data Analytic Team, Scion
    Rotorua, New Zealand

    Data Scientist

    Phenotyping Plateform

    • I am developing mathematical models for phenotyping inferences. I have agglomerated a large dataset of features ranging from topography, lidar measurements, silvicultural, climatic data and genetic information, containing more than one billion observations of the forest. These inferences allow to estimate and predict the productivity of trees across the estate, and therefore to select tree stocks based on environmental conditions for superior genotyping.
      • I designed a machine learning model to assess productivity across a large plantation forest.
      • In charge of a web platform for visualisation of large point-clouds based on lidar acquisition.

  • January 2016 - January 2018

    Centre for Image Analysis and SciLifeLab, Uppsala Universitet
    Uppsala, Sweden

    PostDoc

    Large-Scale Data Analysis for Digital Image Analysis Applications

  • I developed a tool for automated quantification of cell and tissue morphology in digital images. Difficulties lie in the parallel management and processing of 24 images of more than two giga-pixels in size. This framework aims to support clinicians' diagnosis and to complement visual assessment when investigating disease and/or drug response.
    • I developed an open-source tool which combines the analysis of gene expression with quantification of cell and tissue morphology.
    • I shaped a web platform for visualisation of large slide scanner images (e.g., 48000x40000 pixels) at different resolutions.
    • I developed an image processing algorithm for nucleus and lipid droplet segmentation and feature extraction in high-content/high-throughput microscopy screening.

    Characterisation of Volcanic Emissions through Thermal Vision

    • Developed an algorithm to segment and track high-speed particles recorded on thermal videos.
